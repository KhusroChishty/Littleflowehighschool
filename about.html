LITTLE FLOWER HIGH SCHOOL

About Us

Established in 2007, LITTLE FLOWER HIGH SCHOOL was founded with the vision to provide high-quality education at a low cost. Our primary focus is on underprivileged children who have a strong desire to learn but lack access to resources. While many schools charge excessive fees for facilities like computer labs, robotics, sports grounds, libraries, and Islamic education, we are committed to offering these at an affordable price without compromising quality.

We believe that every child deserves the right to learn and grow. Our goal is to empower the poor and underserved communities with knowledge, skills, and values.

Our Key Features

  • Highly Qualified Teachers
  • Personal Attention to Each Child
  • Digital Learning Tools
  • Spoken English Training
  • Monthly Parent-Teacher Meetings (PTMs)
  • Affordable Fee Structure
  • Soft Skills Development Programs
  • Computer Labs with Robotics & AI
  • Islamic Education
  • Karate Coaching
  • Well-equipped Library & Sports Ground
