Haritha Haram – Green Plantation Drive

Posted on: 15th November 2024

On 15th November 2024, our school actively participated in the Telangana Government’s Haritha Haram initiative. Students and teachers together planted over 100 saplings within the school premises, spreading awareness on the importance of trees and green cover. This drive was not just about planting but about nurturing environmental responsibility in our students. We thank everyone for contributing to a greener tomorrow!

Why Education Is Important in Today’s World

Posted on: 10th October 2024

In an ever-evolving world, education is the strongest weapon to face challenges and build a secure future. The Role of Parents in a Child’s Education

Posted on: 28th September 2024

Education doesn't stop at school. Parents play a vital role in nurturing a child’s curiosity and encouraging them to strive for excellence. At our school, we ensure regular parent-teacher meetings (PTMs) to involve parents in their child's journey. From moral support to monitoring progress, the partnership between teachers and parents can make a significant difference in a child’s life and education.
